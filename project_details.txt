- **Input Fields (Advance):** Fire NOC Expiry, Fire Extinguisher Count, Lift Count, Water Tank Count, CCTV Count, Completion Year, etc.
- **Dropdowns (Advance):** 
  - Fire NOC Status (Valid, Expired, Never Applied)
  - Conveyance Deed Status
  - Lift Presence and AMC Type
  - Water Source (Borewell, Municipal, Tanker)
  - Pest Control Frequency
  - Gate Security Type
- **Buttons:** "Save" / "Update" (Redirects to `/admin/dashboard`).
- **Redirection:** Back arrow (Redirects to `/admin/auth` or `/admin/dashboard`).

### 4. Admin Dashboard (`/app/admin/dashboard.tsx`)
- **Labels:** Society Name, Welcome text, Wings/Blocks count, Total Units count.
- **Buttons:**
  - "Profile" (Redirects to `/admin/setup`)

  - "Logout" (Signs out user)
  - "+ Add Wing" (Creates new wing block)
  - Specific Wing Cards (Redirects to `/admin/wing-setup`)
  - "Delete Wing" (× icon on wing card)

### 5. Admin Wing Setup (`/app/admin/wing-setup.tsx`)

- **Buttons:** Generate Structure, Add Floor, Delete Floor, Save Flats.

## Section 2: Project Description & Target Customers

### Project Description
**Zonect** is a specialized, comprehensive society management and administrative platform. Initially focused on digital household staffing records, it provides a centralized architecture to document, manage, and verify all facets of a residential society. The application features two distinct portals:
1. **Admin Portal:** Grants society administrators the ability to define building infrastructure (Wings, Floors, Units), manage statutory safety/compliance details (fire safety, lift AMCs, water logistics), add society-level staff (security, maintenance), and integrate their documents securely using automated Google Drive storage.
2. **Resident Portal:** Allows residents to securely log in using system-generated credentials. Residents can configure their personal profiles, keep track of their family members and vehicles, and, most importantly, register their own personal domestic helpers (maids, drivers, cooks) securely with document proofs.

The system ensures real-time updates and seamless integration with Google Drive to safely store KYC (Know Your Customer) documents and photos of all residents and staff. 

### Target Customers
- **Residential Housing Societies (RWA - Resident Welfare Associations):** Committees looking to digitize their operations, transition away from paper logs, and effectively oversee building structural configuration and compliances.
- **Apartment Complexes and Condominiums:** Large housing networks seeking a robust way to manage security by ensuring strict KYC of all domestic help (maids, cooks, drivers) visiting the premises.
- **Property Managers / Facility Managers:** Companies operating residential locations who require centralized databases for residents, contact information, vehicle registries, and maintenance staff records.
